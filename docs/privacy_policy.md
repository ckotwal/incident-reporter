# Privacy Policy for WireHazard

**Effective Date:** March 30, 2026
**Developer:** Arav Kotwal
**Contact Email:** ckotwal@gmail.com

This privacy policy applies to the WireHazard app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Arav Kotwal (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects your personal information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:
* Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
* The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
* The time spent on the Application
* The operating system you use on your mobile device

### Location Information
The Application requests access to your location data (both precise and approximate). This data is strictly used to display nearby incidents on the map and register new hazard reports accurately. This location data may be uploaded to Google Firebase to track incidents.

### Camera and Media Storage
The Application requires access to your camera and local media storage to allow you to capture photos of incidents and attach them to your reports. Images uploaded within the app are stored securely in Google Firebase infrastructure. 

## Third Party Access
Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help the Service Provider improve the Application and their service. The Application does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, specifically:
- Google Play Services
- Google Analytics for Firebase
- Google Firebase Crashlytics
- Google Maps SDK

## Security
The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information the Service Provider processes and maintains.

## Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy.
